# Risk Rating Model

Threats should be prioritized based on **impact and likelihood**.

## Risk Scoring Matrix

| Impact | Description |
|---|---|
| Low | minimal system impact |
| Medium | moderate system disruption |
| High | major system impact |
| Critical | severe operational or security impact |

| Likelihood | Description |
|---|---|
| Low | unlikely scenario |
| Medium | possible scenario |
| High | likely scenario |

## Risk Calculation

Risk Level = Impact × Likelihood

Example:

| Threat | Impact | Likelihood | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| CI/CD compromise | Critical | Medium | High |
