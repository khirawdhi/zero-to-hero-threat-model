# Attack Analysis

The attacker used social engineering techniques to obtain login credentials from a contractor working with Uber.

Once authenticated, the attacker gained access to internal systems and began exploring the environment.

The attacker identified internal systems that stored credentials or provided administrative access. By leveraging these resources, the attacker escalated privileges and gained access to additional systems.

## Attack Steps

1. Attacker targets employee through social engineering
2. Credentials are obtained
3. Attacker logs into corporate systems
4. Internal tools and documentation accessed
5. Additional credentials discovered
6. Privilege escalation across internal services

## Key Weaknesses

- insufficient protection of internal systems
- excessive access permissions
- lack of strong identity verification for sensitive tools
